1. Product Governance

1.3 Product changes that affect fees or eligibility require Compliance Review approval at least 15 business days before launch.

2. Everyday Checking RBC-CHK-100
2.1 Everyday Checking requires a 25 USD minimum opening deposit.
2.2 The account has a 6 USD monthly maintenance fee.
2.3 The monthly fee is waived when the customer receives at least 500 USD in qualifying direct deposits during the statement cycle.
2.4 The monthly fee is also waived when the average collected balance is at least 1,500 USD.
2.5 A replacement debit card costs 5 USD unless the card was compromised through confirmed fraud.

3. Student Start Checking RBC-STU-110
3.1 Student Start Checking is available to customers age 16 through 24.
3.2 The account has no monthly maintenance fee until the customer turns 25.
3.3 A parent or guardian co-owner is required for customers under age 18.
3.4 Overdraft transfers are disabled by default for Student Start Checking.

4. Premier Savings RBS-SAV-220
4.1 Premier Savings requires a 2,500 USD minimum balance to avoid the monthly service fee.
4.2 As of 2026-01-15, balances from 10,000 USD through 249,999 USD qualify for the standard preferred rate tier.
4.3 Withdrawal monitoring alerts are generated after five outgoing transfers in a calendar month.
4.4 Branch staff must refer rate exception requests to Retail Product Management.

5. Small Business Operating Account RBB-BIZ-310
5.1 The Small Business Operating Account supports sole proprietors and companies with annual revenue below 5 million USD.
5.2 Monthly cash deposits above 25,000 USD require enhanced review by Business Banking Operations.
5.3 The account includes 250 free transaction items each month.
5.4 Business owners must provide beneficial ownership information before account opening.
