Branch Operations Manual
Synthetic evaluation document. No confidential data.

1. Daily Opening and Closing
1.1 Standard branch lobby hours are 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.
1.2 The opening checklist begins at 8:30 a.m. and must be completed before the first customer transaction.
1.3 Vault opening requires dual control by the Branch Manager and a Senior Teller.
1.4 The closing checklist must be signed by the Branch Manager or Assistant Branch Manager.

2. Cash Controls
2.1 The standard teller cash drawer limit is 10,000 USD.
2.2 Vault cash differences greater than 100 USD must be escalated to Branch Operations the same business day.
2.3 Teller overages or shortages above 50 USD require a written variance explanation.
2.4 Night deposit bags must be processed by 10:00 a.m. on the next business day.
2.5 Cash shipments require two employees to verify seals and shipment numbers.

3. Compliance Activities
3.1 Currency Transaction Reports are required for cash transactions above 10,000 USD in one business day.
3.2 Suspicious activity concerns must be submitted to the AML Investigations queue within two business days.
3.3 Branch staff may not tell customers that a suspicious activity review has been opened.
3.4 Safe deposit box access requires identity verification and signature card matching.

4. Customer Service and Records
4.1 Customer complaints must be logged in C360 within one business day.
4.2 The Branch Operations Manager performs weekly reconciliation of dormant account address changes.
4.3 Paper signature cards are shipped to Records Operations every Friday.
4.4 Emergency branch closure notices must be approved by Regional Banking Leadership.
