Bank Recruitment and Workforce Policy
Synthetic evaluation document. No confidential data.

1. Purpose and Scope
1.1 This policy governs hiring for branch, operations, technology, compliance, and risk teams at Northstar Retail Bank.
1.2 The policy is effective 2026-01-01 and applies to permanent employees, fixed-term employees, and paid interns.
1.3 Hiring managers must use the WorkPath requisition system for every role before contacting candidates.

2. Requisition Approval
2.1 A hiring manager opens the requisition and attaches the approved department budget code.
2.2 Talent Acquisition validates the job family, salary band, and interview plan within three business days.
2.3 Finance Business Partner approval is required when the annual base salary is above 120,000 USD.
2.4 The Chief People Officer must approve exceptions to salary bands, location rules, or job grade changes.

3. Candidate Screening and Interviews
3.1 The interview slate must include at least three qualified candidates unless Talent Acquisition documents a scarce-skill exception.
3.2 Branch Advisor B2 roles use a salary range of 52,000 to 68,000 USD.
3.3 Risk Analyst B3 roles use a salary range of 78,000 to 96,000 USD.
3.4 Every candidate for a customer-facing role must complete a conduct attestation before the final interview.
3.5 Interview feedback must be entered in WorkPath within two business days after the interview.

4. Background Checks and Start Dates
4.1 HR Operations owns background checks and employment eligibility verification.
4.2 Customer-facing and risk roles require criminal history, employment history, and sanctions screening.
4.3 A new hire may not access production banking systems until HR Operations marks the background check as cleared.
4.4 Standard start dates are the first and third Monday of each month.

5. Probation, Referrals, and Exceptions
5.1 The probationary period is 90 calendar days for branch and operations roles.
5.2 The probationary period is 120 calendar days for risk, compliance, and model governance roles.
5.3 Employee referral bonuses are capped at 2,000 USD and are paid after the new hire completes 180 days of employment.
5.4 Referral bonuses are not paid for interns, executive roles, or candidates already in the applicant tracking system.
5.5 Policy exceptions require written approval from the Chief People Officer and must be retained for seven years.
