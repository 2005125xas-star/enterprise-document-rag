Data Security and Privacy Policy
Synthetic evaluation document. No confidential data.

1. Data Classification
1.1 This policy is effective 2026-03-01 and applies to employees, contractors, vendors, and service accounts.
1.2 Northstar Retail Bank classifies information as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.
1.3 Customer Social Security numbers, account numbers, debit card PAN data, and authentication secrets are Restricted data.
1.4 Confidential and Restricted data may not be stored in personal cloud drives.

2. Encryption and Access Control
2.1 Restricted data must be encrypted at rest using AES-256 or an approved equivalent.
2.2 Restricted data must be encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 or stronger.
2.3 Multi-factor authentication is required for all workforce access to bank systems.
2.4 Privileged access must be reviewed every 30 days by the system owner.
2.5 Service account credentials must rotate at least every 90 calendar days.

3. Incident Response
3.1 Security Operations owns incident triage and severity assignment.
3.2 The Data Protection Officer owns privacy notification assessment.
3.3 Legal must be notified within 24 hours when a suspected privacy breach involves customer Restricted data.
3.4 Critical security incidents must be reported to the CISO within 30 minutes of confirmation.
3.5 A containment plan for a critical incident must be documented within four hours.

4. Logging and Data Requests
4.1 Security event logs for production banking systems must be retained for two years.
4.2 Access requests for Restricted data must be completed within five business days or escalated.
4.3 Vendor access to Restricted data requires a signed data processing addendum and annual security review.
4.4 Data exports above 10,000 customer records require approval from the Data Governance Lead.
